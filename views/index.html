ATTORNEY FORM BUILDER

ABOUT

Custom legal form creation services

Attorney Form Builder allows lawyers and attorneys to create custom legal forms, such as wills, that customers can then fill out and purchase for on-demand legal document creation, without any waiting periods or the need to schedule an appointment. This is a fast process that saves both attorneys and their clients a lot of time. Legal documents, welcome to the digital age.
